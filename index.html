THE WORLD

CARTESIA

The city that watches.

Cartesia rises in glass and steel, a metropolis of advanced technology in its infancy atop a labyrinth of the forgotten beneath. The Directorate classifies everything from citizens, to government-sanctioned augmentations, to intentions. Below, among the tunnels and time the city has forgotten, a prohibition-style cybernetics black market thrives.

This is a city built on observation. To be seen is to exist. To be classified is to be controlled. And those who slip between the categories? They risk their lives to descend.

The streets hum with the facade of life wrapped in data. The alleys whisper of legends deep beneath. Past the Meridian and in the labyrinth below? A myth is said to pull the strings.

THE MERIDIAN

There's a line in Cartesia you can only cross once. Not on any map. Not enforced by any law. The threshold between the city that sees you and the depths that can swallow your existence.

"Woe unto those who cross the Meridian."

CLASSIFICATIONS

The Cartesian Hierarchy

ABOVE THE MERIDIAN

THE DIRECTORATE

The Cartesian governing body. They observe and establish tight controls, defining and registering what constitutes 'legal' human enhancement.

THE NEXUS

The law enforcement body. Augmented officers patrol the streets. Commanders watch and coordinate from behind the lenses. And somewhere in the deepest shadows, Vigilants — military-grade nightmares connected via the encrypted HiveNet. When you see a Vigilant, it's over.

TRACERS

The spotters. Blending in and unremarkable, they mark potential targets for harvest beneath the city.

CURATORS

Long-game manipulators. Charming and charismatic — a Curator could be a co-worker, the Best Man at your wedding... or your wife. Augmented for psychological expertise, they earn your trust only to sell you out after years of it.

HANDLERS

REAPERS

DEEP LORE

Beneath the music, beneath the story, an architecture waits. Cartesian philosophy splitting mind from body. Freudian descents through the layers of the psyche. Quantum theory where observation collapses possibility into existence and grief as the wave that never resolves. Literary shadows of Frankenstein and Nosferatu woven into the bones. The city itself as metaphor. The albums as maps.

Layers upon layers, easter eggs for those who dig, recursions for those who notice, and revelations for those obsessed enough to piece it all together.
